# It's The Cheapest Option
In a world both like and unlike ours, a group of young adult students all chose the same apartment complex to stay in for their first year of university. They don't know each other, but they'll deal with each other throughout the year!

I hope that hook hooked ya, because I had no idea what I was doing. AA's here to be a flexible setting for me to mess around in. There's no big focus on canon or sequences of events (unless otherwise stated), and it's all for fun.
